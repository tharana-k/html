Data science is a multidisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, algorithms, processes, and systems to extract valuable insights and knowledge from large volumes of data. It combines elements from various domains such as statistics, computer science, mathematics, and domain-specific expertise to analyze and interpret data, enabling data-driven decision-making and predictions. Data scientists leverage tools and techniques to clean, transform, and visualize data, as well as build machine learning models for tasks like classification, regression, clustering, and recommendation systems. Data science plays a crucial role in solving complex problems, improving processes, and uncovering hidden patterns in data across industries such as finance, healthcare, marketing, and more.
